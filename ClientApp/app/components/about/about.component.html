About Me

What I Do

I am a full-stack developer specialized in .NET and Azure-cloud technologies, helping you take your ambitious project to the next level.

What I Can Offer

Regardless of your project's size, I can help you tackle challenges in front- and back-end development using Angular and .NET, elaborate architectural concepts in the context of pure- and hybrid-cloud deployment strategies, and implement them at both an infrastructure and code level.

Whether you need a professional for an one-hour consultation or one that can jump into your boat with some aspiring goals set in advance, I am ready to help you. I keep myself up-to-date with the newest technologies and aspire to use them in my projects every day.
